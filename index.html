Jen AggOwner

I refuse to speak about myself in the third person, as though I didn't write all these bios, so this is awkward. I like building restaurants, my first, The Black Hoof is still the heartbeat of my company, which mostly takes up a small block on Dundas West in Toronto (along with Rhum Corner and Cocktail Bar). In early 2016, with my husband, artist, Roland Jean and Win Butler & Régine Chassagne (of Arcade Fire) we opened Rhum spin-off restaurant Agrikol in Montreal + a little bar next door, ti agrikol. Also, I wrote a book 'I Hear She's a Real Bitch', so it's been kind of a busy year. But it's most important to note that I am, above all, more fun than at first I might appear. Much more fun. I like wine. No. I love wine.
